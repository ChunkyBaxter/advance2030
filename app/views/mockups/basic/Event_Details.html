EVENT DESCRIPTION

Join the Cleveland Professional 20/30 Club and Inside Business magazine as they host the Northeast Ohio’s Top 25 Under 35 Movers and Shakers Awards presented by National City Bank, now part of PNC. The event takes place on Thursday, January 28, 2010 at the Rainforest at Cleveland Metroparks Zoo at 6:00 p.m. Mix and mingle with area young professionals, business leaders and more. In addition, Chris Royane, president, University Circle, Inc. will deliver a keynote address. Tickets are $15 for members and $20 for non-members.
